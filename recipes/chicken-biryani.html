"Chicken Biryani"

Description

Chicken biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines tender chicken pieces with fragrant rice and a blend of spices. This traditional Indian dish is known for its rich taste and is often served at special occasions and gatherings. The chicken is marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, then layered with partially cooked rice and slow-cooked until tender. The result is a deliciously satisfying meal that is sure to impress.

Ingridients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3-4 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt

Steps

  • Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala, garam masala, chopped coriander leaves, chopped mint leaves, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator. Heat 2 tablespoons of ghee or oil in a large pot. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of ghee or oil in a large pot. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes turn mushy.
  • Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is partially cooked, about 10 minutes.
  • In another pot, boil water and add soaked basmati rice along with salt. Cook the rice until it is 70% cooked. Drain and keep aside.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken in the pot.
  • Drizzle saffron milk over the rice for added flavor and color (optional).
  • Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked and the rice is fluffy.
  • Once done, gently mix the rice and chicken together.
  • Garnish with fried onions and chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your delicious chicken biryani!
